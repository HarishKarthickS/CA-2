Flag Master

Flag Master is a game where you have to identify the country that matches the flag shown on the screen. You can test your knowledge and learn about different flags and countries in this fun and educational game. There are three levels of difficulty to choose from, and each level has 10 questions.


  • Easy level: You will see four options to choose from, and you have to select the correct one. You have 5 minutes to answer all the questions. This level is perfect for beginners who want to learn the basics of flags and countries.

  • Medium level: You will see a drop-down menu with all the countries in the world, and you have to select the correct one. You have 7 minutes to answer all the questions. This level is challenging for intermediate players who want to test their memory and logic skills.

  • Hard level: You will see a blank space where you have to type the name of the country. You have 10 minutes to answer all the questions. This level is tough for advanced players who want to prove their mastery of flags and countries.
